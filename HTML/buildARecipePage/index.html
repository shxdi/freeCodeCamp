Banana Bread

This is a recipe for banana bread I found online, its simple and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 0.5 Cup Butter, softened
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1.5 Cups Mashed Banana
  • 2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. Beat sugar and butter in a bowl until smooth. Beat in eggs, then bananas. Add flour and baking soda, stirring just until combined. Pour into the prepped pan.
  3. Bake in the preheated ocven until a toothpick in the center comes out clean, about 1 hour. Remove from the pan and let cool
  4. Enjoy, freeze or refrigerate the leftovers.
